Providing value to Super Camper Enthusiasts

  • This is a new site and will build over time. I expect there will be thousands of plots recorded on here eventually. But we have to start somewhere, right? There are costs to put a site like this on a server and to maintain it so it will be a paid for service.
  • Pioneers: First 200 sign ups who leave a review for a campsite plot will be founder members and it will always be free to use.
  • Second Wave: From user 201 to 500 the site will be free for the first year.
  • And Beyond: The price will be €3 per month or €20 if you pay for a year.

I have designs I'm working on for Mugs, stickers and that sort of thing, whatever camping gear etc. I'm also working on a way to get a discount for members at campsites. I'm open to suggestions for other things that would be of value to you. I'm also open to ideas for the site. I'm a web developer and I'm building this site for you. I want it to be the best it can be.
