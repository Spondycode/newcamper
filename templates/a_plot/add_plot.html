How to Fill in the Form

Title: Whatever you want to call the plot

Description: Add the details of what it is you like about the plot. Such as if it is sunny or with shade. Is it close to the facilities, like the showers, electric hookup and water?

Price: How much does it cost per night

Image: Add a photo to upload

Plot Number: The reference number used by the campsite

What3Words: What3Words is an excellent system to mark a spot easily. The whole of the earth is divided into 3m squares. You could be in the middle of a desert or in forest, well away from any civilisation. Still you can send an address using just 3 words.

Add the complete url including https://

It will look something like this - https://w3w.co/reimpose.pleasures.yowled

IMPORTANT

Especially for the non campsite spots. If you don't add the What3Words address, no one else will be able to find it and you might not find it again either.

Campsite: What is the name of the campsite

Categories: What sort of camping? Three categories to choose from. Wild and that's where ever and without any organisation. The other options are Camping site and Official site but not a camp site. Here in Spain many towns set up a place for passing campervans, RV, and Motorhomes. Be aware some countries don't allow wild camping if you want to put up a tent. Usually due to wild fire concerns, which seems reasonable here in dry Spain.
